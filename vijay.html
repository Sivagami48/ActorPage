C.M-VIJAY

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, widely known by his mononym Vijay, transitioned from a leading Indian cinema superstar into one of Tamil Nadu's most influential politicians. After a celebrated 33-year acting career spanning over 70 films, he founded the Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) party, successfully contesting the 2026 legislative elections and becoming the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu.Cinematic JourneyVijay began his career as a child artist in the 1980s before making his debut as a lead actor in the 1992 film Naalaiya Theerpu, directed by his father, filmmaker S. A. Chandrasekhar. Following a few initial setbacks, he achieved his breakthrough with the 1996 film Poove Unakkaga. He subsequently earned the title "Thalapathy" (Commander) from his massive fan base. Over his three decades in cinema, his on-screen persona evolved from a romantic hero into a vigilante fighting against social injustice and systemic corruption.
